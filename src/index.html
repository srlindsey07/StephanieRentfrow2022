About Me

I am Stephanie Rentfrow and I was born and raised in Gainesville, Florida. Growing up, I always wanted to be a Graphic Designer. In pursuit of my dreams, I attended Santa Fe College majoring in Digital Media Technology. During my time in Santa Fe College, I interned as a Graphic Designer at MediaEDGE Communications. When my internship ended, the company hired me as a a full time Graphic Designer. During my tenure there, MediaEDGE Communications allowed me to grow professionally by giving me tasks that required me to learn HTML and CSS. This is when I discovered how much I enjoy developing user interfaces and fell in love with helping to enhance the overall user experience.

Eventually, Mindtree Limited offered to help me hone the skills that I started to teach myself by offering me a Junior Software Engineer position so that I could learn to develop on the job. Over the years, I have proven myself to be dedicated, determined and eager to learn. To say that I have been fortunate during my career would be an understatement.
